# 📗 User Guide: Souq Al-Yemen Service Marketplace

Welcome! Getting things done has never been easier. This platform allows you to either hire skilled local professionals to solve your daily problems or to register as a Worker and sell your unique expertise.

---

## 🔹 Introduction

**What can you do here?**
- **As a Client/Customer**: You can explore a catalog of pre-designed service packages, message workers natively, and send targeted custom "Hire Me Now" job requests locally!
- **As a Professional Worker**: You can set up a professional portfolio, post various services with custom pricing and imagery, and securely chat with prospective clients!

---

## 🔹 Account Creation & Management

### How to Register
1. Click the **Register** button in the top right corner of the website.
2. Fill in your name, email, and a secure password.
3. Crucial step: Select what you want to do. Choose **"Hire Professionals"** if you are a customer, or **"Work as a Professional"** to become a worker.
4. *Optional*: Click the circular Avatar target to upload a profile picture of yourself before completing the sign up!

### Logging In
If you already possess an account, simply click **Login** in the top menu and apply your email and password. Your dashboard screens will instantly adapt based on the role you chose when registering.

### Changing Settings
If you ever want to update your location, phone number, or change your profile image—log in, click your name in the top right corner, and select **Settings**.

---

## 🔹 Using the Platform

### 🛒 For Customers

**Browsing Services**
Navigate to the **Services** tab at the top of your screen to effortlessly search the entire Marketplace catalog. Click any service card to view its specific details and pricing.

**Hiring a Worker**
Found a professional you trust? You have two options! 
1. **Send Message**: Reach out directly utilizing the internal real-time chat pipeline to ask questions!
2. **Hire Me Now**: Found on any worker's profile, clicking this allows you to fill out a direct Job Request (with your own description, deadline, and monetary budget). It's sent exclusively to them!

---

### 💼 For Workers

**Creating a Service**
1. Navigate to your **My Services** dashboard panel.
2. Click the bright **+ Create New Service** button.
3. Name your service appropriately, define a category (e.g. Plumbing), and write a descriptive summary. 
4. Upload engaging photos of your past work and hit publish! Your job immediately lands on the public board for thousands of clients to see.

**Managing Your Jobs**
Check your **Bookings** and **Requests** tabs frequently! You have complete absolute control to accept custom jobs from clients or decline them effortlessly.

---

## 🔹 Working With Images

Visual proof of skill is everything in a digital marketplace! We allow you to upload images straight from your computer or phone.

### Step-by-Step Upload Instructions
1. In the Service Creation or Settings page, find the image target area.
2. Simply click or tap the box, and your device's file browser will open.
3. Select your desired image file. A tiny temporary preview version will render on your screen!
4. Click `Save` or `Submit`. Our cloud processing pipeline handles securely verifying and hosting your picture format safely.

**Top Tips:** 
- Keep file sizes under 5 Megabytes (5MB). 
- We accept Standard `.JPG`, `.PNG`, and `.WEBP` formats natively.

## 🔹 Common Problems & Fixes

**"My image is not showing!"**
If the page flickers back to a generic stock photo:
- Try refreshing the page! Sometimes the backend pipeline takes half a second to securely generate your URL.
- Ensure your file isn't too large (strictly adhere to 5MB limitations).

**"I cannot log in."**
1. Check that your email is spelled correctly securely. Case sensitivity does not matter.
2. Double check your password!

**"The page crashed or got a funny error."**
This is typically a temporary communication drop with the local server! Just click your web browser's **Refresh/Reload icon**, or press `F5` on your keyboard to instantly synchronize back into the data system securely.

## 🔹 General Marketplace Best Practices

- **Write a Good Description**: If you're a worker creating a service, don't just write "I fix things". Provide bullet points! (e.g., "I provide 24/7 plumbing emergency extraction, specializing with pipe bursts.")
- **Fair Market Pricing**: Review existing platform competitors. Don't heavily undercut, but don't overcharge either. 
- **First Impressions Matter**: High-quality, distinctly clear photos uploaded to your service packages heavily increase traffic statistics and verified bookings natively. Avoid blurry cell-phone shots!
